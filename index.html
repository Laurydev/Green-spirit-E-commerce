They say coffee was born in the highlands of Ethiopia, where a shepherd noticed his goats leaping with energy after eating some reddish berries. Since then, coffee has traveled the world, crossing oceans and cultures, becoming what it is today: a ritual, a pause, a universal language.


In our space, we return to that essence. We seek more than just making coffee — we want to create moments. Every bean we roast, every cup we serve, is crafted for you to find a moment of calm, inspiration, or connection.


Because to us, coffee is not just a drink.
It’s memory, it’s art, it’s presence.
